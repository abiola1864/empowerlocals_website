What Success Means

Creating Wealth

We measure the household wealth increase, revenue growth, profit increase, expenses reduction, better product pricing, and employee expansion of small businesses.

Right Knowledge and Technology

We measure the number of standardized assessments, new online resources, and articles created for small businesses. We also measure the proportion of these knowledge possessed by firm owners.

Eliminating Inefficiency

We measure the level of adherence to new technology and compliance with regulatory requirements to ensure that businesses operate more efficiently.
