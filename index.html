Our theory of change

What success looks like

💰

Creating Wealth

We track revenue growth, profit margins, household income increases, cost reductions, and employee expansion among the small businesses we support.

📱

Right Knowledge & Technology

We measure knowledge retention through standardised tests, digital tool adoption rates, and how many entrepreneurs apply new skills six months later.

⚡

Eliminating Inefficiency

We track compliance with regulations, uptake of digital record-keeping, and how efficiently businesses convert inputs into outputs over time.
