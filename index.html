Our theory of change

What success looks like

💰

Creating Wealth

We track revenue growth, profit margins, household income increases, cost reductions, and employee expansion among the small businesses we support.

📱

Right Knowledge & Technology

We measure knowledge retention through standardised tests, digital tool adoption rates, and how many entrepreneurs apply new skills six months later.

⚡

Eliminating Inefficiency

We track compliance with regulations, uptake of digital record-keeping, and how efficiently businesses convert inputs into outputs over time.

Free on WhatsApp

EmpowerBot —
AI in your pocket.

EmpowerBot is our free AI-powered WhatsApp tool for small business owners. No app to download. No data plan needed beyond WhatsApp.

It delivers personalised business coaching, financial literacy quizzes, recordkeeping guidance, and pricing help — in plain language, on demand, in your chat.

Try EmpowerBot → Learn more

Hi! I want to understand my profit better. I sell provisions.

EmpowerBot: Great! Profit = what you sold − what you paid. If you bought garri for ₦2,000 and sold it for ₦2,600, your profit is ₦600. Want me to help you track this daily?

Yes please! How do I start?

EmpowerBot: Every morning, write: what you bought and the cost. Every evening, write: what you sold and for how much. I will help you calculate the rest. Ready?

Free on WhatsApp · No download needed

How we work

Evidence before everything.

We survey before we train. We measure before we claim. We stay after everyone else has left.

01

Survey first

We visit communities and markets before writing a single training module. 2,000+ firms surveyed across Lagos.

02

Design with data

Every training session, tool, and handbook is built from what our data shows entrepreneurs actually need.

03

Measure learning

We test participants before and after every programme. We track behaviour change months later, not just attendance.

04

Stay in the field

Monthly farm visits. In-person coaching. EmpowerBot on WhatsApp. We don't train and disappear.
