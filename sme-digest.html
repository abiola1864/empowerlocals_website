What is in it

98 pages built for the real Lagos market.

The SME Digest was written after three years of surveys with nearly 2,000 small business owners across Lagos. Every chapter addresses something our data showed entrepreneurs actually needed — not generic finance advice, but specific guidance for people running market stalls, provision shops, tailoring businesses, and small trade operations.

It is free. It always will be.
