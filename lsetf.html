About the programme

Agege MSME Bootcamp

Delivered across five consecutive Thursdays from March to April 2026 in Agege Local Government Area, this bootcamp brought hands-on financial literacy, digital skills, and business management training to over 250 micro-entrepreneurs.

Each session was designed around standardised assessments — so we measure what entrepreneurs actually learned and retained, not just attendance.

🤝 In partnership with LSETF
01

Session 1 — March 26, 2026

Business Fundamentals & Recordkeeping

Separating personal and business finances, setting up a simple cash book, and understanding why records matter for growth.

02

Session 2 — April 3, 2026

Pricing, Costing & Profit Margins

Hands-on costing exercises covering cost-of-goods-sold, breakeven pricing, and setting profitable prices without losing customers.

03

Session 3 — April 10, 2026

Digital Tools for Small Businesses

Introduction to EmpowerBot on WhatsApp, mobile banking, and digital invoicing — with live demonstrations on participants' phones.

04

Session 4 — April 17, 2026

Access to Finance & Credit Literacy

Navigating LSETF loans, cooperative savings, and understanding interest rates — practical guidance tailored to Agege market realities.

05

Session 5 — April 23, 2026

Business Planning & Graduation

Participants presented mini business plans, received certificates, and completed standardised knowledge tests to measure programme impact.

What we measured

Learning outcomes

📊

Knowledge retention

Standardised pre- and post-assessments measured actual learning — not just attendance or satisfaction scores.

📱

Digital adoption

Tracked uptake of EmpowerBot and mobile banking tools among participants 30 days after the programme ended.

💬

Peer recommendation

96% of participants said they would recommend the programme to another entrepreneur in their community.
