Who we are looking for

We work with small businesses and farming communities that are ready to grow but lack access to the right knowledge, tools, or capital. We are particularly looking for:

✓

Micro-entrepreneurs in Lagos markets who have no formal recordkeeping system

✓

Small business owners who have attended government training but want evidence-based follow-up

✓

Farming communities in Niger State or nearby states with structured land access and community leadership

✓

Women-led businesses in underserved local government areas

✓

Entrepreneurs who use WhatsApp daily and are open to digital tools
