Mission

---

Small businesses will have access

to the right knowledge and technology

to drive economic progress

and generate positive social impact.

Vibrant local economy

We measure the revenue growth, profit increase, expenses reduction, better product pricing, and employee expansion of small businesses.

Right knowledge and technology

We measure the number of standardized assessments, new online resources, and articles created for small businesses. We also measure the proportion of these knowledge possessed by firm owners that are made available to stakeholders.

Operating with Best Practices

We measure the level of adherence to new technology and compliance with regulatory requirements to ensure that businesses operate with the best practices, with guidance and support provided by ELP.
