What we do

We go to the market.
Every time.

ELP Foundation's Market Outreach Programme is not a training event. It is a long-term commitment to being present in the communities where small businesses operate — Lagos markets, Agege streets, and wherever local people are building something.

Since 2019, our field teams have surveyed more than 100 markets across 15 local councils in Lagos State. We have spoken with nearly 2,000 entrepreneurs. We have learned far more than we have taught.

The findings from those surveys shaped everything else we do: the SME Digest, EmpowerBot, the LSETF bootcamp curriculum, and our ongoing research on digital record-keeping.

The Market Outreach Programme

In December 2022, ELP Foundation ran its most visible outreach to date across Lagos markets: field surveys, distribution of the free SME Digest, hands-on training sessions, and one-on-one consultations with business owners.

The programme was covered by Nigerian Tribune and ThisDay. Television crews were present. For many entrepreneurs, it was the first time an NGO had come to their market rather than asking them to attend a workshop in a conference room.

We brought the training to the stall, not the other way around. That distinction matters more than most people realise.
