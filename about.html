Empowering Local Businesses

Eliminating inefficiencies in local businessess through technology, knowledge, and sustainable business practices since 2018

Our Vision

A vibrant local economy where small businesses have access to knowledge and technology that eliminates inefficiencies, driving economic progress and positive social impact in remote communities.

Our Mission

Supporting small businesses with data-driven research, innovative technology, and essential tools for success, creating a culture of learning and collaboration.

Our Beneficiaries

Serving entrepreneurs with an average age of 36, majority being women (58%), and a median daily profit of ₦20,000 ($12).

Our Journey

2017: Foundation

Established with a vision to empower micro-businesses in Nigeria

2018: Official Registration

Registered with CAC as a limited trustee, began formal operations

2019: Expansion

Launched comprehensive training programs and built a network of 30+ volunteers

Present Day

8,000+ businesses trained, AI-powered mentoring platform launched, partnerships with major institutions
