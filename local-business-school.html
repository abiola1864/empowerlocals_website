Our approach

Not a workshop.
A system.

Most NGO training is a day long. Participants attend, receive a certificate, and return to their business unchanged. ELP Foundation's Local Business School is different because of how we designed what to teach — not just what we teach.

We ran surveys across 100+ markets before writing our first module. We tested participants before and after every session. We measured what changed months later, not just at the end of a training day. Every resource we produce is free of charge — a direct output of that process.
