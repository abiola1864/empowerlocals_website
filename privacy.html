Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Who we are

Empowerment for Local People Foundation (ELP Foundation) is a registered non-governmental organisation in the Federal Republic of Nigeria. We operate empowerFarmers, EmpowerBot, and MSME training programmes across Lagos and Niger State.

What data we collect

We collect personal data including name, email address, phone number, and general location when you submit forms, make a donation inquiry, nominate a beneficiary, or contact us directly. We do not collect payment card information directly.

How we use your data

Your data is used to: respond to your enquiries, process donation commitments, send programme reports and updates, and improve our programmes. We do not sell, share, or rent your data to third parties.

Donor data

Donor names and contact details are used solely for programme administration. We may share your name (not contact details) in programme publications with your prior written consent.

Programme participant data

Data collected from training participants and farmers (names, locations, financial data) is used for programme monitoring and evaluation. It is shared with research partners only in anonymised form unless explicit consent is given.

Cookies

This website uses minimal cookies for basic functionality. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking tools.

Your rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. Contact info@empowerlocals.org to exercise these rights.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions, email info@empowerlocals.org or write to: 41 CMD Road, Magodo Phase 2, Lagos State, Nigeria.
