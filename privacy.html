Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2024

1. Introduction

Empowerment for Local People Foundation ("Company", "we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Empowerment for Local People Foundation.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information about you in a variety of ways when you use our Service. This includes information you provide directly to us, information that is collected automatically, and information from other sources.

2.1 Information You Provide

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, upload content, or communicate with us. This information may include your name, email address, phone number, and any other information you choose to provide.

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

We automatically collect certain information about you when you use our Service, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and other usage information.

3. Use of Information

We use the information we collect in various ways to provide, operate, maintain, and improve our Service. This includes using the information to:

  • Provide and deliver the services you request
  • Process transactions and send you related information
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and requests
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities
  • Personalize and improve the Service

4. Sharing of Information

We may share information about you as follows or as otherwise described in this Privacy Policy:

  • With vendors, consultants, and other service providers who need access to such information to carry out work on our behalf
  • In response to a request for information if we believe disclosure is in accordance with, or required by, any applicable law, regulation, or legal process
  • If we believe your actions are inconsistent with our user agreements or policies, or to protect the rights, property, and safety of Empowerment for Local People Foundation or others

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

6. Your Choices

You may update, correct, or delete information about you at any time by logging into your account or contacting us. If you wish to delete or deactivate your account, please contact us, but note that we may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@empowermentlocal.org
