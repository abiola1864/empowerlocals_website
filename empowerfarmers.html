Who we are

An initiative of
ELP Foundation.

empowerFarmers delivers interest-free agricultural inputs, agronomic training, and evidence-based field support to smallholder farmers in Niger State. We go to the communities — on the land, with the farmers, every planting season.

We survey every community before a single input is given. We measure every harvest. We stay for the whole season — monthly farm visits, no exceptions.

Every farmer receives

A full kit. Before the rains. Not after.

01

Certified Seeds

SC651 hybrid maize seeds — higher yield, faster maturity, optimised for Minna soil.

02

NPK Fertilizer

Two 25 kg bags per acre. NPK 20-10-10 for correct basal application.

03

Neem Coated Urea

One 50 kg bag. Slow-release urea for top-dressing — more nitrogen reaches the crop.

04

Lagoon Herbicide

Full season weed control. Clears the field in the critical first 10 weeks.

05

Labour Cash Grant

Cash for land preparation so farmers clear and plant on time without waiting for credit.

06

Agronomic Training

Monthly in-person field visits from planting through to harvest storage.

How it works

The Patience Model

01

Patient Capital

No interest. No fees. No pressure during the growing season. We wait for the harvest. You invest. They grow. Then they contribute.

02

Research First

We survey every community before a single input is given. Garatu and Taidna were surveyed April 2026. We build from evidence, not assumptions.

03

In-Kind Repayment

At harvest, each farmer contributes 5 mudus (~5.65 kg) of grain back to the community fund. Small enough to feel nothing. Meaningful enough to fund the next family.
